Our Monetization Model

We believe in empowering KJs. That's why our professional software is completely free to use. Our revenue comes from a simple, transparent partnership that helps you earn more.

The Software is Free. Seriously.

The KJ-Nomad application is 100% free for KJs. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no feature limits. Our goal is to give you the best tool for the job without adding to your costs. We only make money when you do.

How We Make Money: A Win-Win Partnership

We integrate a seamless tipping system into online mode. When singers tip you through the app, we keep a 10% commission.

Our built-in features are designed to encourage singers to tip more often and in larger amounts than they normally would. The result? You take home more money at the end of the night, even after our commission.

The Tipping Engine: More Tips for You

These features are designed to maximize your earnings. All of them are optional and can be turned off if you don't want to use them.

  • Gentle Nudge A simple, non-intrusive option for singers to add a tip when they request a song.
  • Social Recognition ❤️ Singers who tip get a heart next to their name on the rotation screen, encouraging others to tip.
  • Tip to Skip Allow singers to tip for priority in the queue - a powerful earner for busy nights.
  • Tip Prize Raffle Gamify the experience by offering an end-of-night prize draw for singers who tip, building excitement and loyalty. You choose the prizes - drinks vouchers, branded merch, or even cash!
  • Gated Features Restrict certain features, like YouTube requests, to singers who have tipped.

Seamless Payments & Payouts

We make the money part easy and secure for everyone.

  • Easy for Singers We accept major payment methods including Debit/Credit Cards, PayPal, Venmo, and Cash App.
  • Automatic for KJs Your earnings are automatically deposited to your bank account. We support Direct Deposit (ACH) and Payout to Debit Card.
